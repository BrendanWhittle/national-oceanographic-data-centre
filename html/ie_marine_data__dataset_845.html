Research Vessel Underway Data

Complete dataset of Underway observations and measurements include sea surface temperature (Thermosalingraph), sea surface salinity, fluorescence, meteorological features including air temperature, atmospheric pressure, atmospheric humidity, wind speed and direction from the BATOS weather station, depth to seabed measurement and vessel navigation measurements including Heave, Pitch, Roll, Heading and Speed.
